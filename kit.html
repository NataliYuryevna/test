What is Mobirise Kit?

Mobirise Kit is a service that provides the access to all current and new themes/extensions developed by Mobirise for 1 year. New themes/extensions are released every month and will be available in your account during one year, without any additional charge.

Can I cancel any time?

You can cancel your Mobirise plan at any time with a single click in your account.
If you cancel your plan, you will have the access to Mobirise theme/extensions until the day your next plan payment was due to be billed. For example, if you cancel your annual plan 4 months before your payment is due, you will still get access for another 4 months.
After your plan has expired you will still be able to work with the projects created before expiration.

What happens to my sites if I don’t renew?

Your existing sites will remain completely functional in your account, even if your 1-year plan is expired. However you won't be able to create new sites with premium themes and will lose the access to new releases, updates, and support.

Can I access extensions on another computer?

You can use the extensions/themes on up to 2 computers under the same Mobirise account. So, if you logged in with the same Mobirise account (email) on your home and work computer, your extensions will be available on both.

Can I use themes on multiple websites?

Yes, you can use themes/extensions from the kit on an unlimited number of websites, including your own or clients' websites.
